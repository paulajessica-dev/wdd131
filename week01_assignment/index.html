About Me

My name is Paula Jessica Silva, but you can call me Paula. I was born in Brazil and I live with my family in Fortaleza, Ceará. I currently work as a Business Intelligence Analyst at a mining company. I have been married for almost 18 years, and my only child, Mateus, is 13 years old. I love spending time with my family, and we enjoy visiting the beautiful beaches of Ceará together. I am a persistent person, and learning new things and overcoming challenges are what make life more exciting for me.

Fortaleza, Ceará

Ceará is a state in northeastern Brazil known for its beautiful beaches, sunny weather, and vibrant culture. Fortaleza, the capital city where I live, is famous for its coastline, local food, and friendly people. Some of the most popular tourist destinations in Ceará are Jericoacoara and Canoa Quebrada, which are known for their breathtaking landscapes and relaxing atmosphere.
